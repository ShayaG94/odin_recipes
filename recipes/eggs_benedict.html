Eggs Benedict

Home

Description

These Eggs Benedict use fresh mozzarella cheese and prepared hollandaise sauce so your favorite brunch dish is on the table is just minutes.

Ingerdients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 2 English muffins, toasted
  • 10 fresh basil leaves
  • 8 oz. Mozzarella Fresca
  • 2 sliced tomatos
  • 2 poached eggs
  • 4 tablespoons prepared hollandaise sauce
  • Chopped fresh chives
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  • Step 1
    1. Butter the muffins
    2. Top with the fresh basil
    3. Add some Mozzarella Fresca slices
    4. Add a tomato slice
    5. Add the poached egg
  • Step 2
    1. Drizzle with hollandaise
    2. Add some chives
    3. Add salt and pepper to taste
    4. SERVE IMMEDIATELY!
